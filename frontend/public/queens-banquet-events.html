Our Story

More Than Events,
We Create Memories

Founded in 2012, Queens Banquet Events began with a simple belief: every celebration, no matter the scale, deserves meticulous attention and genuine passion. From a small team of three, we've grown into a premier event management company serving Metro Manila and beyond.

Our philosophy is rooted in personalization. We don't just execute events — we listen, understand, and translate your unique story into an experience your guests will talk about for years.
